One Registry. All Guitars. Forever.

The $8 billion vintage guitar market relies on scattered, unreliable documentation that leaves collectors, appraisers, and enthusiasts guessing about authenticity and provenance. When legendary instruments change hands for hundreds of thousands of dollars, everyone deserves better than forum posts and fading memories.

The Guitar Registry solves this by creating the world's first permanent, cross-brand database where every guitar's complete story lives forever. Through cryptographically-signed attestations, experts, owners, and witnesses can permanently attach their knowledge to specific instruments, creating unalterable records that enhance value and preserve heritage.

We're not just building a database—we're preserving cultural history while creating new economic value for everyone in the guitar ecosystem, from bedroom collectors to major auction houses.
